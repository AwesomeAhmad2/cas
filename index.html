Sandhurst

Activity During the summer, I was sponsored by the MiSK organization to travel to the Sandhurst Royal Military academy for the purpose of primarily developing my leadership and follower ship skills. During my time there, we got lectured and was put through a rigorous physical assault course which cadets there took on a daily basis. The course built-on our teamwork skills and exposed us to a stressful environment where we had to corporate in order to succeed. It also gave us an outlook on struggles that cadets must undergo to graduate from the academy. I found the course to be challenging because I lacked the physical capacities required for the course. We also did hands-on team training activities to develop our leadership skills, specifically with our teams.
